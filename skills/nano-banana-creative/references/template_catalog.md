## How to use

When constructing a brief for Nano Banana:

1. **Select** a template that matches the Strategy (e.g. "Urgency" -> `bfcm/1.png`).
2. **Reference** it in the prompt: *"Use the layout structure from 'assets/visual_templates/bfcm/1.png' but replace the clock with a modern smartwatch."*
